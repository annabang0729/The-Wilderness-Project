Steps for Registration

1. Fill Out Registration Form

Applications are submitted through the First Year Center and are accepted on a frist-come, first-serve basis. Registration starts May 6th. Upon acceptance into our program, you'll receive an email confirmation.
Click here to apply.

2. Complete Supplementary Forms

After receiving our confirmation email, students must review the participant packet and sign attached medical and policy forms. They're required.
Click here for the forms.

3. Prepare!

View our Packing List to make sure you have everything you need for the trip! The more prepared you are, the better off you'll be.
